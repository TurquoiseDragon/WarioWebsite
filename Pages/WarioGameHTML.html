WARIO'S GAMES!

Despite being the antagonist in his first appearence Wario has many games over the years! look and see if you recognize any!

Wario Land Series!

Wario Land: Super Mario Land 3

Wario Land was the sequel to Mario Land 2 and was the first game to feature wario as a protagonist.
In this game wario seeks out to build his own castle to rival Mario's

Virtual Boy Wario Land

Wario Land on the virtual boy is an odd title in the series but follows many of the principles present in Wario Land 1.
In this title wario seeks outs the treasure of Awazon and truly starts to grow into the role of a treasure hunter.

Wario Land 2

Wario Land 2 Is the title that wario starts really becoming a puzzle platformer, remarkably being the first title to ditch damage and opt for puzzle solving transformations by getting hit with certain attacks.
this game follows wario trying to take back his treasure from his nemesis Captain Syrup.

Wario Land 3

Wario Land 3 Expands upon the gameplay of Wario Land 2 while also adding in a large array of levels that are affected by your progress throughout the game, adding not only puzzles into the levels but also to the world map itself.
This Adventure follows Wario finding a magical music box he is sucked into and must find a way out of.

Wario Land 4

Wario Land 4 marks the return of damage to the Wario Land series along with a new gimic to the levels of Wario Land 4, a getaway timer. every level in wario land 4 follows wario escaping the level before it collapses by rushing back to the start after they find a key.
Wario Land 4 has wario find a mysterious pyramid in a jungle that he finds himself trapped and must escape with the treasure hidden inside.

Wario World

Wario World is the first and only 3D Wario Land game featuring entirely new gimmicks and combat! the game has no special gimics other than 3D combat and puzzles in vain to previous Wario Land titles.
Wario World sees wario trapped inside a world created by an evil gem with the only way out is defeating the gem and collecting his now transformed treasure.

Wario: Master Of Disguise

Wario: Master Of Disguise is the black sheep of the Wario Land series featuring not much similar gameplay to other Wario Land aside from basic actions. The rest of the game features wario putting on several disguises to progress through levels in a similar vain to previous transformations.
This title follows Wario in a rivalry with The Silver Zephyr as they fight for treasure.

Wario Land Shake It!

Wario Land Shake It! is the most recent title in the Wario Land series and plays very similarly to Wario Land 4, the main differences being the introduction of shaking. Wario is now able to shake anything he grabs for more cash or for health.
This game follows Wario in the Shake Dimension where he fights to the Shake King to eventually claim the "Bottomless Coin Sack".

WarioWare Series!

WarioWare, inc: Mega Microgames!

This WarioWare game is the first in the series. This game is a compilation of high paced "microgames" with reflex and puzzle based challenges thrown at the player at a rapid pace. This game also features many original characters exclusive to the WarioWare titles.
This game and many to follow it follow Wario trying to make big bucks by selling these microgames to people with him ultimately losing it all due to his own greed.

WarioWare: Twisted!

WareWare: Twisted! started a trend that remained a key point in the WarioWare series, that being game exclusive gimics. these gimmicks would be the core of many of the new microgames present in each title. This title featured the physical moving of your gameboy to affect the game.
this game features many of the same characters as the previous title with a very similar plot.

WarioWare: Touched!

WarioWare: Touched! continues the trend of gimic based microgames by using the DS's touchscreen to expand the library of microgames.
This title's plot is very similar to previous titles with the exception of more character oriented stories.

WarioWare: Smooth Moves

WarioWare: Smooth Moves introduces the Wii's motion controls to the WarioWare series with many interesting and new ways to use the Wii-mote.
This game features many new characters with a similar character oriented story like WarioWare: Touched!.

WarioWare: Snapped!

WarioWare: Snapped! is a relatively tiny game with only 20 microgames but uses the DS's camera to great affect to produce interesting games.
This game has very little plot but does also only include a fraction of the characters present in previous games.

WarioWare D.I.Y.

WarioWare D.I.Y. is nintendo's first "game maker" game where in the player themselves make the games. the player is able to create their own microgame and music and upload it onto, the now defunct, Nintendo Wi-Fi.
D.I.Y.'s plot follows Dr.Crygor, a character in previous WarioWare game, inventing a way to make microgames and Wario trying to make money off of it in similar vain to previous titles.

Game & Wario

Game & Wario is the first and only WarioWare title to ditch the naming convention. This game does not follow the conventions of previous games and contains 12 larger micro games all of which using some aspect of the Wii U and having infinite versions.
Game & Wario follows many of the story convetions of previous titles with character based stories and wario attempting to make money off of his games.

WarioWare Gold

WarioWare Gold is a compilation of nearly all previous WarioWare titles contaning microgames from nearly every single title minus Snapped and D.I.Y. due to their unique gimmicks.
In this game Wario organizing a tournament for his microgames that is eventually proved to be one big scam by Lulu, a new character in the WarioWare series.

WarioWare: Get It Together!

The most recent title in the WarioWare series this game has microgames that feature the WarioWare characters themselves solving each microgame with their own abilities.
This game follows the WarioWare characters being sucked into one of Wario's Games as they try and escape.

Wario SpinOffs Series!

Wario Blast: Featuring Bomberman!

Wario Blast: Featuring Bomberman! is the only game that doesn't follow any of the conventions of the other two series and instead plays like bomberman. The player is able to play as Wario in this title but controls identically to bomberman.
Despite not having much plot this game is assumed to be the start to Wario's association with explosives.
