WELCOME TO WARIO'S WEBSITE!

This Website is dedicated to the our favorite yellow doppelganger... WARIO!

Many people of Wario but not as many know his history and just how many games our money grubbing crook is in!

Look below to see where your favorite version of wario comes from!

Standard Wario!

The version of Wario you know and love! originally appearing in Super Mario Land 2 his dashing style hasn't changed much throughout the years (no point in fixing perfection!).

You'll find him sporting this attire is most modern games and any of those akward tennis sessions with mario and pals along with any solo adventures nowadays!

Truely one of the rouge's best outfits!

Biker Wario!

The version of Wario you might remember from Smash but he first wore these cool duds back in the first WarioWare title!

With his branded helmet and dashing denim jacket this wario is prepared for battle, prepared for the road, and prepared to make a quick buck with some microgames!

If this outfit is lame then wario doesn't want to be cool!

Adventure Wario!

This attire comes from a need to explore! originally appearing in Wario Land his hat is synonymous with riches and excitement!

His cap makes sure Wario is ready to seek out treausre wherever it may be and allows him to transform into... a dragon?! no matter how crazy the adventure Wario will always be prepared with this outfit

Despite his hat being hung up in more recent years this outfit is more than iconic!

Lets delve more into who this handsome devil is!
